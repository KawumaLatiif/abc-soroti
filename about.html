About ABC BlendEd

ABC BlendEd, initiated in 2008 by European universities, is a blended mobility challenge-based project course designed to foster innovation. Supported by an EU Capacity Building grant through ATHENA, it adapts this model for African universities in agriculture, aiming to create a global BlendEd community. The consortium includes 11 partners, such as Polytechnic Institute of Porto, University of Siegen, Hellenic Mediterranean University, RUFORUM, Odisee, and six African universities including Soroti University, with the Portuguese Association of Young Farmers as an associated partner.

Objectives

Foster student employability and add value to local communities by designing and deploying a tailored BlendEd course for African agriculture.

Encourage adoption of BlendEd courses among students, teachers, and companies through content, infrastructure, and training development.

Promote widespread adoption in Africa via a Community of Practice empowering local stakeholders.

Our Mission

To foster cross-border collaboration among students, transcending disciplines and nations, and create impactful projects for local communities.

Our Vision

To enhance soft skills, global perspectives, and networks, preparing students for the challenges of today’s job market.

Our Motto

"Learning by Doing, Growing by Serving"

About Soroti University

Soroti University, established in 2015 in Eastern Uganda, is committed to quality education and innovation. It emphasizes community connections and practical education to address local challenges.

Vision

“A University of professionals and innovators”

Mission

“To provide knowledge, skills and innovations for inclusive sustainable development and transformation”.

Core Values

  • Team work - We support each other, prioritizing team over individual performance.
  • Integrity - We act in harmony with our deeply held beliefs and values.
  • Inclusiveness - We respect diversity in opinions, race, gender, faith, abilities, and ideologies.
  • Responsiveness - We respond to clients in a timely manner.
  • Creativity - We bring imagination to life.

Goal

“To be a widely recognized community-oriented University that brings together theoretical knowledge with practical education that impact on social and economic development”.

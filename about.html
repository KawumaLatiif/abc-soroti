About ABC BlendEd

Empowering the next generation of innovators through blended learning and community-driven solutions

What We Do

ABC BlendEd is an innovative educational initiative at Soroti University that combines traditional learning with cutting-edge technology to address real-world challenges in our community. We believe in the power of blended education to create sustainable development and develop the next generation of problem solvers. Our programme focuses on hands-on learning experiences, where students work directly with community stakeholders to identify research and develop solutions to local challenges. Through this approach, we bridge the gap between academic theory and practical application.

Our Mission

To empower students with skills, knowledge, and enthusiasm that address community challenges while fostering sustainable development.

Our Vision

To be the leading blended education program in East Africa, driving positive change in communities and beyond.

Our Motto

"Learning by Doing, Growing by Serving"

About Soroti University

Soroti University is a leading institution of higher learning in Eastern Uganda, committed to providing quality education that is relevant to the needs of our society. Established in 2015, the university strives to become a center of excellence in education, research, and innovation. The university prides itself on its strong community connections and practical approach to education, addressing local and regional challenges while maintaining high standards of academic excellence.

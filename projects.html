Challenges 2024 Edition

As part of the ABC BlendED project (Erasmus+ Project ID: 101128996), the 2024 edition focuses on designing and piloting BlendED courses in Agriculture. These challenges address local community needs, enhancing student skills in teamwork and innovation through iterative design and evaluation processes.

Challenges 2025 Edition

The 2025 edition aims to refine the BlendED model, expanding its application across African universities. It will build on pilot results to ensure sustainability and generate outputs like community projects and an active Community of Practice.

Our Projects

Removal of Astringent Taste from Cashew Nuts

This project focuses on developing methods to eliminate the astringent taste from cashew nuts, enhancing their market value and consumer appeal through innovative processing techniques.

Land Suitability Analysis for Oil Palm and Cassava

The initiative conducts a detailed analysis to determine suitable lands for oil palm and cassava cultivation, aiding farmers in optimizing agricultural productivity and land use.

Improving Maize and Soybean Quantities in Angonia

This project explores strategies to enhance the supply of maize and soybean from farmers to the feed processing factory in Angonia, boosting local agricultural output.

Promoting Livestock Feed Production in Angonia

The project aims to promote production and conservation of livestock feed, addressing the scarcity of natural pasture grasses, especially during summer when grazing lands are limited.
